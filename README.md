# EU_Real_Estate_FP20_Challenge_36

The project, is a comprehensive data analysis of over 5,000 European real estate listings. It utilizes advanced visualization tools, likely Power BI integrated with ZoomCharts, to identify volume and pricing benchmarks across multiple countries including Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, and Spain.

# Key Analysis Pillars

# Inventory & Market Composition: 
A massive 5,000-property sample size providing a statistically significant view of the market.

# Structural breakdown: 
65% Residential, 28% Commercial, and 7% Mixed-Use.

Regional coverage across 10+ countries, with deep dives into high-activity zones like Spain, France, and Germany.

# Performance & Benchmarking:

# Financials: 
Established an average sales benchmark of €1.05M with an average rental yield of 4.17%.

# Liquidity: 
Average "Days on Market" is 172 days, serving as a critical KPI for investor exit strategies.

# Volatility: 
A high price deviation of €1.27M indicates a market with significant luxury outliers, requiring the 3-month smoothing techniques applied in the report.

# Spatial & Asset Intelligence:

# Geospatial Mapping: 
Using OSM to visualize capital flows, showing a concentration of high-volume investments in major hubs like Barcelona and Berlin.

The "Green" Premium: Analysis shows only 11.4% of properties are eco-certified (Energy Rating A/B), identifying a significant supply gap in the energy-efficient building sector.
